This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of our website. By using this site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. 📋 Information We Collect

We operate a privacy-first, no-backend tool. Here is exactly what we collect and do not collect:

What we DO NOT collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • The financial figures you enter into the calculator (assets, home value, income)
  • Your legal situation details (custody, alimony, asset disputes)
  • Any personally identifiable information through the calculator itself
  • Credit card or payment information (the tool is free)
  • Your location beyond what your browser voluntarily provides to Google for ads

What we may collect through third-party services:

  • Anonymous usage data via Google Analytics (page views, time on site, device type) — if enabled
  • Ad interaction data via Google AdSense (whether you viewed or clicked an advertisement)
  • Technical information such as browser type, operating system, and referring URL (standard web server logs)

3. 💾 Calculator Data & localStorage

To provide a better experience, the calculator saves your inputs (such as state, years married, home value, and expense amounts) in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Your data is stored only on your own device — it never leaves your browser
  • We cannot access, read, or retrieve this data — it is invisible to us
  • The data persists between sessions so you can return to your scenario without re-entering everything
  • You can delete this data at any time using the "Reset" button in the calculator, or by clearing your browser's localStorage in your browser settings
How to clear your calculator data manually:
Chrome/Edge: Settings → Privacy and Security → Clear browsing data → Cached images and files / Site data
Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Clear Data → Cached Web Content
Safari: Preferences → Advanced → Manage Website Data

6. 🍪 Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. divorcecostbreakdown.com uses the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: The calculator uses browser localStorage (not traditional cookies) to save your inputs. localStorage is technically distinct from cookies — it stores data locally on your device and is not transmitted to any server.
  • Advertising cookies: Google AdSense sets cookies on your device to serve relevant advertisements. These are set by Google, not by us. They may include the DoubleClick cookie, which Google uses to track ad impressions, clicks, and conversions.
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics sets cookies to track aggregate, anonymous usage statistics.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the display of advertisements on this site but will not affect the calculator's functionality (which uses localStorage, not cookies).

10. 🌴 California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with additional rights regarding your personal information.

Because divorcecostbreakdown.com does not sell personal information and does not collect personal information through the calculator itself, many CCPA provisions do not apply in the traditional sense. However, we are committed to transparency:

  • We do not sell your personal information to any third party
  • We do not share your calculator inputs with any third party
  • Google AdSense's use of cookies for advertising purposes may constitute "sharing" under CCPA's expanded definition — you can opt out via Google's Ad Settings
  • To exercise your CCPA rights, contact us and we will respond within 45 days
